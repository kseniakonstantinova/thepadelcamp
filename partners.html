Sponsorship Opportunities

We have designed flexible packages to meet your marketing objectives.

Match Point Partner

Ideal for local businesses looking to support the community.

  • Logo on our "Supporters & Partners" webpage
  • Social media thank you post
  • Brand visibility at the venue
  • Small logo printed on the backside of the participants t-shirts
Game Set Partner

Perfect for regional brands seeking direct engagement.

  • Includes all Match Point benefits
  • Logo on all digital communication (email signatures, results sheets)
  • Inclusion of promotional materials in participant welcome packs
  • Social media stories featuring your brand during the event
Tournament Partner

The ultimate partnership. Be the name on everyone's lips.

  • Includes all Game Set benefits
  • Title Sponsorship of the "Americano Tournament" on the final day
  • Prominent logo placement on the front side of the t-shirt of all participants
  • Opportunity for a representative to present the awards
